TEAM & EXPERIENCE

HiFu was founded by Iggy Zhang and Dong Chen in the U.S. who collectively have over 24 years of experience in cross-border commerce, marketing, payments, mobile technology, and merchant partnership at HP, Visa, American Express, and PayPal. Other key members of the team include two senior advisors, Tom Shepard and Richard Chang, both are former senior executives with global portfolio at Visa Inc., and two very seasoned Sales veterans with a combined 60 years of U.S. retail industry experience.
